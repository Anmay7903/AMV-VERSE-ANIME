Synopsis

Mahavatar Narsimha tells the divine story of Lord Vishnu's fourth avatar, Narsimha - the half-man, half-lion incarnation who appeared to protect his devotee Prahlad and restore cosmic balance. The movie depicts the epic battle between Narsimha and the demon king Hiranyakashipu, who had gained a boon making him nearly invincible.

Set in ancient times, the film explores themes of devotion, dharma, and the triumph of good over evil. With stunning visual effects and powerful performances, Mahavatar Narsimha brings this sacred story from Hindu mythology to life for modern audiences.
